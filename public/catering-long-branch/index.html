What Gigi's Caters

We cater the full Gigi's menu: hand-stretched New York-style pizza, Grandma squares, and specialty pies, plus hot and cold heroes, pasta, salads, appetizers, and full Italian dinners. It travels well and feeds a crowd.

Feeding guests with dietary needs? We make gluten-free NY-style pizza (prepared in a shared kitchen, so not guaranteed allergen-free) and vegan pizza on our NY crust with dairy-free cheese and vegetable toppings.
