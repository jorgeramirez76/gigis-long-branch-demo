Gluten-free NY-style, any toppings

Order a gluten-free pie the same way you'd order any Gigi's pizza. We start with a gluten-free crust and top it however you like — classic cheese, pepperoni, fresh veggies, or one of our specialty combos.

Going plant-based too? Our dairy-free vegan cheese works on the gluten-free crust, so you can build a pie that's both.

An honest note on our shared kitchen

We want you to order with clear expectations. Our gluten-free pizzas are prepared and baked in the same kitchen as our regular pies, so we can't promise a fully allergen-free or gluten-free-certified pizza.

If you have celiac disease or a serious gluten allergy, please tell our staff when you order. We'll take extra care, and you can decide what's right for you.

Good to know

Is Gigi's gluten-free pizza safe for celiacs?

Gigi's gluten-free pizza is made on a gluten-free crust, but it's prepared and baked in a shared kitchen, so it isn't certified allergen-free and there's a risk of cross-contact. If you have celiac disease, please tell our staff when you order so we can take extra care.

Can I get any toppings on the gluten-free crust?

Yes. The gluten-free crust works with our full range of toppings — cheese, meats, vegetables, and specialty combinations. You can even use our dairy-free vegan cheese to make it both gluten-free and vegan. Order online or call (732) 377-2468 if you'd like help building your pie.

Yes. Gigi's offers pickup and delivery seven days a week. We deliver gluten-free pizza across Long Branch, the West End, Pier Village, Elberon, West Long Branch, Monmouth Beach, Deal, Eatontown, Allenhurst, and Oceanport. Order at gigislongbranch.com or call (732) 377-2468.
