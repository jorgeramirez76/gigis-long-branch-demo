What we collect

  • VIP Club signups: your name, and the phone number and/or email you choose to share, along with the consent language you agreed to and a timestamp.

Our texting promise: No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never sold, rented, or shared with anyone for their marketing.

Who processes data for us

We use trusted service providers strictly to operate the business: Clover (order and payment processing), Twilio (sending our text messages), Resend (sending our emails), and our website/database hosting. They process your information only on our instructions and never for their own marketing.

Your choices

  • Texts: reply STOP anytime to any message — removal is immediate. Reply HELP for help.
  • Emails: every promotional email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.
  • Everything else: call us at (732) 377-2468 or email ggigispizzalb@gmail.com to ask what we have or to request deletion.

Data retention & security

We keep VIP Club and order records only as long as needed to run the program and meet legal/tax obligations. Access is limited and protected.

Children

Our site and messaging programs are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted here with a new effective date.
