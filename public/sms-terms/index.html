SMS Program Terms — Gigi's VIP Club

Effective July 22, 2026

Gigi's VIP Club is a recurring text-message program from Gigi's NY Style Pizza, 140 Brighton Ave, Long Branch, NJ 07740. Messages are sent from (848) 275-3977.

What you'll receive

Promotional offers, weekly specials, promo codes, and occasional service messages related to your VIP Club membership. Message frequency varies — typically up to 4 messages per month.

How you joined

You (or someone with access to your phone) opted in on our website at gigislongbranch.com by entering your phone number and checking the "Text me deals" box. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. We only text people who signed up themselves — we never buy, rent, or import phone lists.

Opting out

Reply STOP (or STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT) to any message and you'll be removed immediately. You'll get one final message confirming the opt-out. You can rejoin anytime by replying START or signing up again on our website.

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile plan. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

Privacy

Your mobile number and opt-in consent are never shared with third parties or affiliates for their marketing. See our Privacy Policy for details.
